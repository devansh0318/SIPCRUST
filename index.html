Artisanal Roots

The Hearth & The Brew.

"We believe the soul of a community is found in the warmth of a wood-fired oven and the steam of a morning cup."

The Hearth

Sourdough as an Obsession

Every crust at SIPCRUST begins with a wild-yeast starter we've nurtured since our first day. We don't believe in shortcuts. We believe in the slow, 48-hour fermentation process that develops complex flavors and a structure that is both crisp and ethereal.

Our wood-fired ovens reach 900 degrees, blistering the sourdough into a 'leopard-spotted' masterpiece in mere minutes. It is a dance of heat, flour, and time.

Explore our pizzas

The Brew

Coffee for the Soul

A great meal shouldn't end when the last slice is gone. Our coffee program is built on the same artisanal principles as our kitchen. We source seasonally-fresh green beans from small-holder farms, roasting them in small batches to highlight their unique origin profiles.

From the bright acidity of an Ethiopian pour-over to the velvety texture of a latte made with house-processed oat milk, every cup is a celebration of the craft.
